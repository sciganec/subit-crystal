# Matrix‑64

The 64‑state matrix is the full expansion of the crystal into its complete semantic space.  
Each row corresponds to a primary trigram, each column to a secondary trigram.  
Every cell is a unique 6‑bit archetype formed by combining two spectral phases.

---

## Trigram Order

100 Blue  
101 Green  
110 Lime  
111 Yellow  
011 Orange  
010 Red  
001 Burgundy  
000 Purple

This order is invariant across all crystal structures.

## Interpretation

### Primary trigram  
Defines the dominant energetic mode (row color).

### Secondary trigram  
Defines the modulating energetic mode (column color).

### Combined state  
The 6‑bit archetype is the minimal structural identity of the state.

Matrix‑64 is the complete visible surface of the crystal.  
All semantic, structural, and energetic configurations of SUBIT‑crystal emerge from this grid.
